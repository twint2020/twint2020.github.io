I’m Ted Wint. At Gold House Ventures in San Francisco—the definitive early stage fund in North America built to back Asian Pacific-led startups—I lead platform initiatives that support our portfolio of founders and amplify GHV’s cultural and strategic impact. As the venture arm of Gold House, GHV invests in and champions Asian Pacific founders shaping the future of culture and commerce.

Previously, I was a founding member and operations lead at a q-commerce startup, where I launched and scaled a rapidly growing global urban fulfillment network that unlocked new markets for consumer brands across the U.S. and Canada. Before that, I led an operations and strategy team at Winc, a modern winery with a direct connection to the consumer, and before that, I managed operations at Tapcheck Inc., an early-stage FinTech startup.
